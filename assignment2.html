"It ain't over till it's over"- Yogi Berra (May 12, 1925-September 22, 2015)

Yogi Berra is one of the greatest baseball players of all time. He played for 19 seasons as catcher with the Yankees. He was a 15 time all star, a 3 time MVP, and he is one of 4 players to do that. He was inducted to the Baseball Hall of Fame in 1972.
